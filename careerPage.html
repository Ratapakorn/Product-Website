Where would you like to work?

HQ and leadership

At the heart of our wood-crafted company, our HQ and Leadership team is dedicated to driving sustainable growth and fostering innovation in the art of woodworking. As stewards of our mission and values, they oversee everything from strategic planning and operational excellence to creative direction and environmental responsibility. This team ensures that every piece we craft embodies our commitment to quality, craftsmanship, and sustainable practices, while leading a passionate workforce that’s united by a love for fine woodwork and design.

Warehouse, Store & Customer Service

Our Warehouse, Store & Customer Service team is the backbone of our operations, ensuring that our handcrafted wood products reach customers with the highest standards of care and quality. In the warehouse, our team meticulously handles each item, maintaining inventory, and preparing orders for delivery. In our stores, knowledgeable staff showcase the beauty of our crafted pieces, helping customers find the perfect addition to their homes. Our Customer Service team provides friendly, personalized support, answering questions, and addressing needs to ensure an exceptional experience. Together, this team brings our craftsmanship directly to customers, creating lasting connections and satisfaction.

Who we are.

We are on a mission to improve lives and strengthen communities. At our wood-crafted company, we believe in creating beautiful, sustainable pieces that bring warmth and character to homes, while also giving back to those in need. Part of our mission? Supporting underprivileged families by dedicating a portion of our earnings to help provide essential resources. Our commitment to sustainability is reflected in our circular approach—minimizing waste and reusing materials wherever possible. We believe in crafting with purpose, fostering a world where people own thoughtfully and share generously.
